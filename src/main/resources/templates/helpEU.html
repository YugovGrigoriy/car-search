1. With this page, you can view specific characteristics of cars and compare them with each other (currently only Ford vehicles are supported).

  1. 1.1 Please write your query without spelling errors, as this may affect the search results or even lead to an error.
  2. 1.2 The query should include:
    1. The make of the car
    2. The model of the car
    3. Vehicle generation
    Typos or the use of unreliable information in the query (such as specifying an incorrect year of production) may result in incorrect results.
  3. 1.3 The search result for a specific model displays the top-end equipment of this car.

2. You can add a car to your favorites to quickly access it when needed.

  1. 2.1 Saved cars will be displayed in your personal account.

3. Car comparison is based on two parameters: horsepower and cost.

  1. 3.1 For some cars, the cost may not be indicated, and such cars are not available for comparison.

4. To register, you need to provide a unique login (username).

  1. 4.1 The password must meet certain conditions:
    1. Must not contain the login
    2. Must be at least 8 characters long
    3. Must not contain simple sequences ("12345") ("qwerty")
    4. Must include at least 1 lowercase letter, 1 uppercase letter, and 1 digit

5. At the bottom of the main page, you can report an error by clicking on the corresponding button

  1. 5.1 In the error report, please briefly describe where the error occurred and how it is manifesting.
